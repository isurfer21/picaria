Help & Instructions

Objective: Be the first player to get three of your marks (X or O) in a straight line—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Gameplay Flow

  1. Player X (You): Starts the game and makes the first move.
  2. Computer (Player O): After your move, the computer AI will analyze the board and make the best defensive or offensive move.
  3. Winning Condition: The game ends immediately when a winning line is formed or when all cells are filled (a Draw).
