Game Guide: Understand Picaria

🎯 Core Objective

Be the first player to achieve three marks (X or O) in a consecutive line—whether it's horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

⚙️ How to Play

  1. Your Turn (Player X): You always start the game. Click on any empty space to place your mark.
  2. AI's Move (Player O): After you place your mark, our built-in AI analyzes the board and makes its best strategic move.
  3. Winning/Draw: The game immediately concludes upon winning (a line of three) or when all nine squares have been filled (a Draw).

💡 Pro Tip: Pay attention to both defensive and offensive moves. Blocking your opponent before making a move is often the key to victory!
